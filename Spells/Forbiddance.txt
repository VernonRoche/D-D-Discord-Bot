***Forbiddance***
**Level: **6th
**Casting Time: **10 Minutes
**Range: **Touch - (40,000ft2)
**Components: **V, S, M *
**Duration: **1 Day
**School: **Abjuration
**Attack/Save: **None

You create a ward against magical travel that protects up to 40,000 square feet of floor space to a height of 30 feet above the floor. For the duration, creatures can't teleport into the area or use portals, such as those created by the gate
